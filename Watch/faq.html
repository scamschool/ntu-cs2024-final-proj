Step-by-step setup:

  1. Charge your Smart Band 10 fully before first use
  2. Download the Mi Fitness app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  3. Open the app and create a Xiaomi account (or sign in if you have one)
  4. Tap the "+" icon and select "Band"
  5. Choose "Smart Band 10" from the device list
  6. Wake up your band by pressing the side button
  7. Follow the on-screen pairing instructions
  8. Grant necessary permissions (Bluetooth, notifications, etc.)
  9. Complete your personal profile for accurate health tracking

The entire setup process takes about 5 minutes.

If you're having trouble pairing, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
  • Make sure your band is charged (at least 20% battery)
  • Keep your phone within 0.5 meters of the band during pairing
  • Restart both your phone and Smart Band 10
  • Forget any previous Bluetooth connections to the band in your phone settings
  • Update the Mi Fitness app to the latest version
  • Reset your band: Go to Settings > System > Factory Reset

Important: Don't pair the band through your phone's Bluetooth settings. Always use the Mi Fitness app for pairing.

Note: Your health and activity data will sync to your Xiaomi account, so it's accessible from any device once you sign in.

Firmware updates bring new features and improvements. To update:

  1. Ensure your band has at least 20% battery
  2. Connect to Wi-Fi (recommended) or stable mobile data
  3. Open the Mi Fitness app
  4. Go to Profile > Smart Band 10 > Firmware Update
  5. If an update is available, tap "Download and Update"

The app will notify you when updates are available.

The Smart Band 10 uses an advanced PPG (photoplethysmography) sensor that provides medical-grade accuracy for resting heart rate measurements.

Accuracy factors:

  • Wear the band snugly (1-2 fingers width above wrist bone)
  • Keep the sensor area clean and free from sweat/moisture buildup
  • For best results during workouts, tighten the band slightly
  • Tattoos or scars on the wrist may affect accuracy

The band monitors your heart rate continuously and alerts you if it detects unusually high or low readings.

SpO2 (blood oxygen saturation) measures the percentage of oxygen in your blood. Normal levels are typically 95-100%.

The Smart Band 10 can measure SpO2:

  • On-demand: Manually check anytime through the device
  • During sleep: Automatic monitoring to detect breathing irregularities
  • During workouts: Track oxygen levels during intense exercise

How to take a measurement: Stay still, keep your arm at heart level, and wait 30 seconds for results.

Note: This feature is for fitness purposes only and not intended for medical diagnosis.

The Smart Band 10 uses advanced algorithms to automatically detect when you fall asleep and wake up, tracking:

  • Sleep stages: Deep sleep, Light sleep, REM sleep, and Awake time
  • Sleep quality score: 0-100 rating based on duration and stage distribution
  • Sleep breathing quality: Detects breathing irregularities
  • Heart rate during sleep: Tracks resting heart rate patterns
  • Nap detection: Recognizes daytime naps over 20 minutes

View detailed sleep insights and personalized suggestions in the Mi Fitness app each morning.

The Smart Band 10 measures your stress level using heart rate variability (HRV) analysis, providing a score from 1-100:

  • Relaxed (1-29): Low stress, good recovery state
  • Normal (30-59): Balanced stress levels
  • Medium (60-79): Elevated stress, consider taking a break
  • High (80-100): High stress detected

Features:

  • All-day automatic stress monitoring
  • Breathing exercises to help reduce stress
  • Stress alerts when levels are consistently high
  • Weekly stress trends and patterns in the app

Battery life varies based on usage, but typical performance includes:

  • Typical use: Up to 14 days
  • Heavy use: 7-9 days (always-on display, frequent GPS workouts, continuous health monitoring)
  • Power saver mode: Up to 21 days

Battery-draining features:

  • Always-on display
  • Frequent notifications
  • Continuous SpO2 monitoring
  • GPS-enabled workouts
  • Maximum brightness settings

Adjust these settings in the band or Mi Fitness app to optimize battery life.

The Smart Band 10 uses a magnetic charging cable:

  1. Align the magnetic charging pins on the cable with the contacts on the back of the band
  2. The magnets will snap into place automatically
  3. Plug the USB end into a power adapter, computer, or power bank
  4. The band will vibrate and show charging animation
  5. Full charge takes approximately 1.5-2 hours

Tips:

  • Clean the charging contacts regularly with a dry cloth
  • Use a 5V/1A or higher USB adapter for best results
  • You can use the band while it's charging

If you're experiencing rapid battery drain, try these solutions:

  • Turn off always-on display: Settings > Display > Always-on display
  • Reduce brightness: Swipe down > Adjust brightness slider
  • Disable continuous SpO2: Mi Fitness app > Device settings > Health monitoring
  • Limit notifications: Only enable essential apps
  • Update firmware: Ensure you have the latest software
  • Restart the band: Settings > System > Restart
  • Factory reset (last resort): Settings > System > Factory reset

If issues persist after trying these steps, contact Xiaomi Support for assistance.

Power saver mode extends battery life by disabling non-essential features. When enabled:

Disabled features:

  • Heart rate monitoring (except manual checks)
  • SpO2 monitoring
  • Stress monitoring
  • Sleep tracking
  • Automatic screen wake

Active features:

  • Time display
  • Basic step counting
  • Alarms
  • Manual workout tracking

When to use: Activate power saver mode when your battery is low and you won't be able to charge soon, or during extended trips.

To enable: Settings > Battery > Power saver mode
