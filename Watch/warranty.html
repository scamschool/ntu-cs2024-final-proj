Official 12-Month Limited Warranty

Your purchase of the Xiaomi Smart Band 10 is backed by our local support team and a comprehensive warranty.

1. Warranty at a Glance

Item Warranty Period Scope of Warranty
Smart Band 10 Main Device (Tracker Unit) 12 Months Manufacturing Defects (Parts & Labour)
In-Box Accessories (e.g., Charging Cable) 6 Months Manufacturing Defects (Replacement)
Purchased Accessories (Straps, Pendants) 6 Months Manufacturing Defects (Replacement)

Warranty period starts from the date of the original purchase.

2. What the 12-Month Limited Warranty Covers

This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship for the Smart Band 10 Main Device under normal use circumstances.

  • Failure of the core functionality (e.g., non-responsive screen, charging failure, sensor malfunction) not caused by external factors.
  • Manufacturing defects that prevent the device from performing its advertised functions.

3. What Is NOT Covered (Warranty Exclusions)

The warranty is void and does not cover the following:

  • Damage resulting from misuse, accident, or abuse (e.g., drops, crushing, fire, flood).
  • Damage caused by unauthorized repairs or modifications performed by third parties.
  • Normal wear and tear, including scratches on the screen/casing or natural depletion of battery capacity over time.
  • Damage from use outside the 5ATM water resistance specifications.
  • Use of non-original or unauthorized accessories.
  • Products without a valid proof of purchase (original receipt/invoice).

4. How to Submit a Warranty Claim

  1. Troubleshoot First: Check our FAQ/Troubleshooting page as many issues can be resolved quickly.
  2. Prepare Information: Have your Original Order Number and a detailed description of the fault ready.
  3. Contact Local Support: Email our Singapore-based support team or use the live chat function:

    Email: support@xiaomihelp.sg

    Hotline: 800 852 8474 (Mon-Fri, 9am-6pm SGT)

  4. Assessment: Our team will guide you through the return process. Once approved, the device will be repaired or replaced.

5. Your Consumer Rights in Singapore

The benefits provided by this limited warranty are in addition to, and do not affect, any other rights and remedies a consumer may have under the Consumer Protection (Fair Trading) Act - CPFTA (Lemon Law) of Singapore.

You have the right to claim for a defective product sold within six months of purchase, as per Singapore law.
