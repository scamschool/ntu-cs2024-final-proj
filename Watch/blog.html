Where the Tech Comes From

Xiaomi's multi-channel PPG module was co-developed with Huami Health. By splitting the light spectrum into four discrete wave-lengths, the sensor cancels motion artefacts 3× better than the previous generation.

The result? Medical-grade SpO₂ deviation of ±1.2% and heart-rate accuracy within 2 bpm—validated against Polar H10 chest straps.
